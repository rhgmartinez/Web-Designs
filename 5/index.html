ABOUT US

Here at Toothless' Metalworks, we pride ourselves with creating the finest crafts, including weapons, armor, and building materials. Hosting a smithy renowned across the Island of Berk, our blacksmith shows his skill and dominance of the forge. Our services are available for public and private purchases.

   Once the dragons of Berk were accepted into society, the founder, Toothless settled down and set up shop. He first only crafted for his closest friends, but his expertise and natural talent at the forge soon brought on the attention of customers across the Island.

FAQs

  • Q: Where do you acquire your metal?
  • A: All of our metal components are mined and refined right here in our lovely Island of Berk.

  • Q: How many smiths are there?
  • A: We have a multitude of apprentice smiths from across the island and surrounding areas, but none as skilled as Toothless himself.

  • Q: Are there positions available?
  • A: We're always looking out for bright potential students, so feel free to send us an application for an apprenticeship over here!

  • Q: How expensive are your wares?
  • A: We boast the most fair prices in Berk, as each piece is up for barter at the time of sale.

  • Q: Where can I find your store?
  • A: The address of our shop can be found here! Stop by anytime! :)

  • Q: Do you smith custom armor and weapons?
  • A: Yes, we do! At the time of the order, we will fit you for your armor and forge off those measurements. Weapons can also be custom forged by request.

  • Q: How old is your store?
  • A: Our head smith Toothless founded this establishment almost as soon as dragons became welcome as friends and family in Berk.
