    FAQs: Frequently Asked Questions



    What is metalcrafting?



    Metalcrafting is the art of executing artistic designs in metal

    What are metals?



    Metals are materials that are hard, opaque, shiny, and has good electrical and thermal conductivity. Metals are generally malleable which means they can be hammered or pressed permanently out of shape without breaking or cracking.

    What kind of dragon is Toothless?



    Toothless is a Night Fury. He is presumed to be the only living Night Fury, at least on Berk and the surrounding Isles.
